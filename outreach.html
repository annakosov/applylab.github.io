Outreach Events

Are you a future vision scientist?

We value giving back to the community and showing the world cool science! At the APPLY Lab, we teach the local community about how our perception of the world works. This spans from showing visual illusions like scanimations, to using inverted goggles to slip the world upside down to solve mazes and play cornhole.
